TRADITIONAL PILATES: CONTEMPORARY METHODS

While the traditional principles will be the foundation of what we do it is important to progress with the industry to ensure our clients are always receiving the latest methods of training. Classes are programmed with the client in mind with each and every exercise having purpose - a moment is never wasted during class.
With high level of instruction, small group classes and a focus on posture, flexibility, function and technique you are guaranteed to leave feeling refreshed and invigorated. 

1

MOVEMENT

Start by simple steps, and later you will be surprised by your progress.

2

TIME

Spend forty minutes for your training, it is enough to start.

3

PRACTISE

Take two or three workouts a week to maintain your level.

4

WEIGHT LOSS

Achieve your goals, it will be hard sometimes, but it is worth it.

STOP WISHING. START DOING.

We believe you should truly enjoy moving your body. Classes are programmed with the client in mind to ensure you are continually progressing in your movements. With our high level of instruction, small group classes and a focus on posture, flexibility, function and technique you are guaranteed to leave feeling refreshed and invigorated.
